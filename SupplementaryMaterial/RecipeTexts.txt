Almond Cookies:
Ingredients:
230 g butter, room temperature
120 g sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp almond extract
2 1/2 cups flour
1 cup almond flour
1/4 cup powdered sugar

Instructions:
Beat the butter and the sugar together until light and fluffy.
item Add the vanilla and almond extracts and mix.
item Add the flour and almond flour.
item Mix thoroughly. 
item Take generous tablespoons of the dough and roll it into a small ball, about two cm in diameter, and then shape it into a crescent shape.
Place onto parchment paper lined baking sheet
Bake at 175 C for 15--20 minutes.
Dust with powdered sugar.
